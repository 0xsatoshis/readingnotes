	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这一些事务累加在一起，又促成了一个“房票交易市场”。也就是我可以帮你用票，目前报酬大约是房价的5%&nbsp;<a name="_ftnref4" style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(96, 127, 166); text-decoration: underline; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 10px;" title=""><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 18px; font-size: 10px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">[4]</span></a></span></p>

	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">七）总结</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">交易的成本一共有四样：税费+限贷+限购+压价成本。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">其中前三样的金额是：税费9%+限贷3%+限购5%+中介费1%=18%</span></p>
