				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">士族在《三国演义》小说中，只不过是一个名字。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">但历史的实质，远远比这更复杂。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">七）文士</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">曹操讨袁的时候，有一个叫陈琳的人，写了一篇《讨曹檄文》。骂曹操祖宗十八代都是太监，骂得十分不堪。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">而这样的文章，只有陈琳能写。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">袁绍手下虽然谋士如雨，但是田丰，沮授、审配、逢纪、荀谌，郭图、许攸、辛评，每个人都有自己的事情干。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">对于骂娘这种事，实在兴趣不大。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<blockquote style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 10px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; border-left: 3px solid rgb(219, 219, 219);">
					<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
						<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">我们知道，&ldquo;内廷&rdquo;中的，都是神仙。</span></p>
					<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
						<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">外庭，都是高官&nbsp;</span></p>
					<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
						<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">参谋，带兵大帅。</span></p>
					<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
						<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">士族，清流世外。</span></p>
				</blockquote>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这样七推八推，最后&ldquo;檄文&rdquo;就流落到一些文学词藻，小字辈的手中干活。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">杨修杨主簿，如果出现在官渡之战中，肯定也很适合干这样的事。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">相对来说，文官们都是&ldquo;干活&rdquo;的。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">而卖弄文字，则被视为&ldquo;杂耍&rdquo;的。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">例如郭沫沫在D内地位从来都不高，大伙都看不起他一个执笔文人。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">虽然人家自称精通诗词歌赋，文章达练。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">而且肯舍得拉下脸皮拍马屁。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					&nbsp;</p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">八）结语</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&ldquo;谁是最厉害的文官&rdquo;，这种问题，本身就是没有答案的。你说一个人能力强，那是没用的。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">1）内廷。内廷主要比的是，和天子的亲密度</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">2）外庭。主要看政绩和资历。爬升缓慢。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">3）</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体; color: red;">参谋，这是唯一可以迅速决出谋士优劣的渠道。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">4）士族，士族靠的是血统。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">5）文士，弄臣。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">文官这个概念，本身就是五种职业，五种纬度。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">因此比较谁优谁劣，谁聪明谁毁身，本身就是毫无意义的。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">&nbsp;</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">相对来说，比较女演员的颜值，要更容易一些。</span></p>
				<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
					<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">譬如这部戏里面，我选甄宓。</span></p>
