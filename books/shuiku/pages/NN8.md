			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">法家本身是一件“吃毒药长内力”的事情。秦二世在和平年代严厉压迫，不仅破坏了生产力，而且一旦发生民变，把整个秦王朝都端了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">法家的真正对立面，是“黄老”。萧何曹参文景之治，名闻天下的“无为而治”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">黄老的意义，就是官家不折腾，不搞事。连续N年不给县令摊派任务。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">让人民可以充分地休息。从事一些长期投资的项目，从而长远获得肌体生命力。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">法家光辉的代表人物，是诸葛亮，李元昊。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这二人都是以一州一府之地，将国力压榨到了极致。以一城和天下抗衡，甚至将大国逼压着打。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">但值得注意的是，法家并不意味着诸葛武侯治下的百姓生活过得好。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">“</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">独尊儒学”在刘邦建国时代就已经确立了基础。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">“体，用，术”。无论法家还是黄老，他们都是第二层面的事情。都是“用”这级。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">“儒术”才是根本。儒奠定了等级制国家组织形式这一基本基础。在此基础上，天子是征战四方（法），还是修养生息（黄老），那是第二层级的事。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">其后董仲舒上书汉武帝“废黜百家，独尊儒术”。这根本就不算事，甚至都不算是改动。因为儒术是底层规则。而其他的墨，法，黄老，鬼谷子，纵横家，都是上一层的应用层面学术。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">他们本身就和儒术不构成冲突。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">三）大学</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">在汉宣帝时期，继续发生了有趣的对话。也就是董仲舒约50年后。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">太子刘奭（汉元帝）对父亲说：“父亲应该多亲近儒生，这可是治国安邦的人才”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">宣帝正在吃饭，听着赶紧把嘴里的饭吐到盘子上。直接说：“儒生只会夸夸其谈，一点真实本事没有。这样的人怎么可以依靠”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这段话的讽刺之处。它每一句都是真的。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">儒生的确是夸夸其谈，儒生的确是什么都不懂。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">既不懂得工程，也不懂得算术，更不懂得农桑。“百无一用是书生”。丫站在庙堂上，就是个文盲。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">可真正讽刺的，正是这些“什么都不懂”的儒生。却是国家的栋梁。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">我们知道，之后的二千年岁月，基本就是这些“百无一用”的书生，垄断了中国的官场。皇帝给他们官做，搞了科举。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">你要说这些熟读“圣人之言”的学生们，到底学了些什么，那可真是什么都不懂。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">一个秀才，下不懂农桑不辨五谷，上不识水利不懂盐铁。那这样一个儒生还有什么用。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">可皇帝偏偏还要把官给儒生做。基本上“官、吏”分开。凡是官这一类职务，全都给儒生做了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">到后来更是发展到不是儒生，就不能做大官。至少在“进士”这一栏，是卡得很严很严的。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">你没有一个进士的出身，在官场就受排挤。能活下去就很不错了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">整个官场从上上下下全部都塞满了儒生。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">那么，请问儒生可以做什么事?</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">儒生其实什么事也做不了。任何事都得交给手下的人去做，干吏去做。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">儒生们只能做一件事，那就是“全力维持这个体制”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">每一个进士公务员，都是等级制的最忠实支持者。所以皇帝让他们塞满每一个官爵。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">孔子说，“大学之道，在明明德”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">什么叫“大”学，这就是大学。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">另外顺便吐一下槽。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">说起中国的好大学，向来都是北清复交。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">复旦交大是“综合性大学”，培养高等人才的。听起来很霸道。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">我自己就是复旦出来的，说实在真没觉得什么好。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>

		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">

		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这种思想，简直就是为“考状元”的人度身定做的呀。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">禅宗讲究“顿悟”。顿悟的另一方面，则是不可以血统继承，也不能师徒继承。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">哪怕你被雷劈中，但你不能保证下一次谁被雷劈中。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">不能保证你的儿子，或者你的徒弟，也能“顿悟”。因为顿悟是灵气，不能学习。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这种宗教思想，简直就是为官僚制度度身定做的呀。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">中世纪的Pope最憎恨的就是教士们犯淫戒，偷偷地生私生子。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">因为教士们有私生子后，他们就会尽可能地把权力和职位传给私生子。而不是权力归留于教皇。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">对于中国的皇帝，也是同样的道理。皇帝并不欢迎大臣们把权力传给自己的儿子或者徒弟。所以禅宗也是皇室最喜欢的宗教。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">“禅宗”是儒术的副产品。因为有了儒教等级制，才有向上流通的需求。才会有顿悟和禅宗。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">五）喇嘛教</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">说到这里，我们也不难解释另一个学术疑问。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">“</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">为什么在内地势如破竹地禅宗，最终在西藏遭到了挫败。桑耶寺之辩，禅宗彻底输给了渐悟，西藏从此陷入喇嘛教怀抱”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">桑耶寺之辩是宗教史上的著名事件。公元776年，顿渐二派在藏北桑耶寺举行了长达二年的辩论。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">辩论最终以渐悟派胜出。禅宗淡出西藏，喇嘛教取得主流。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">中国的知识分子，一方面惊讶于禅宗在华区的战斗力，一方面惊讶于禅宗在西藏的失败。一方面为西藏没有溶入中国文化圈而惋惜不已。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">其实这有什么好奇怪的。你要搞清楚，西藏和内地的根本不同。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">西藏，他是一个佛教国家（/地区）。他奉行的是政教合一制度，当权的是喇嘛!</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">在西藏，喇嘛本身就是统治阶级。实行活佛与寺庙制度。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">好了，现在你一个小喇嘛跳出来，说“我有神的指引，我顿悟了，我要做上师指导你们前行”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">你想一想，方丈老禅师会怎么想，怎么做，怎么动。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">他还不一掌劈死你！</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">在西藏，本身就是政教合一制度。喇嘛掌握政权，老喇嘛就意味着资历，官职和“论资排辈”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">所以西藏只能够采取“渐悟”制度。你侍奉佛祖的时间越长，职位就越高，级阶就越高。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">如果人人都跳出来喊“我顿悟了”。岂不是天下大乱。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">很多人对西藏的“活佛转世制度”有所误解。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">西藏有3000多个活佛。但其实，除了最顶层的达-赖，班禅之外，活佛的转世并不是随机的。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">一般而言，一些中小型的活佛，其转世的半径，不会超过50KM。或者更确切点说，永远都在村委书记家。一般都是由村长的堂兄堂弟“幸运”地转世为活佛。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">或者在一个县里，由几个大家族，“轮流”转世成活佛。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">你稍微有点常识想一想，在当时的生产力下，也不可能翻越几座大山，到300KM以外去找转世证据。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">甚至更有一种说法，连达-赖本身的转世，其8，9，10，11世也是可疑的。因为他们都是转世在大贵族家。而且不到20岁就被谋-杀。达-赖真正转世在贫民之家的次数极少。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">所以，与其说西藏是喇嘛统治，政教合一。活佛其实还有一个更好的翻译法：“土司”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">只不过不知那些进藏洗涤心灵的女文青们怎么想了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">在内地，骨架是儒教“等级制”。禅宗作为一种科举考状元的思想，作为补充，YY出人头地。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">而在藏区。喇嘛本身就是统治阶级。所以他们断断不会允许你“顿悟”的。哪怕历史再辩一百次，也是渐悟胜出。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
