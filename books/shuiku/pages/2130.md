	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">第七类，汽车。汽车4S店其实利润十分微薄。某人返0.6%就把整个经销商体系搅乱打垮了。</span></p>
