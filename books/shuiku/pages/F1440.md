<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">太子舍人不是正式的官职。想要入<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">仕</span>，门槛自然低很多。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">而司马懿的目光长远，曹丕一旦即位，他就是&ldquo;小时候的老兄弟&rdquo;。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">真正读懂史书的人，才知道&ldquo;太子&rdquo;是最值得投资的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">历朝历代，太子一旦即位，&ldquo;舍人&rdquo;都贵不可言。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">年羹尧，田文镜，李卫，当初都是雍正的府客。雍正如果不夺嫡，他们连史书上留下名字的机会都没有。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">事实证明，司马懿果然是&ldquo;聪明绝顶&rdquo;。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">在曹丕和曹植的斗争中，司马懿屡出奇计，勾心斗角。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹丕登基之后，他也顺理成章拿到了御史中丞，开始进入中央。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹叡的母亲，甄夫人，原本是袁熙的正妻。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">美艳不可方物，被俘后一跃从俘女，变成曹丕的正妻。后来的文昭皇后。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<blockquote style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px 0px 0px 10px; max-width: 100%; border-left: 3px solid rgb(219, 219, 219); color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 25.6px; widows: 1; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

	<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 40px;">
		<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 40px; font-family: 微软雅黑, sans-serif; color: rgb(152, 72, 6); box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">凌波微步，罗袜生尘，光润玉颜。</span></p>
	<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 40px;">
		<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 40px; font-family: 微软雅黑, sans-serif; color: rgb(152, 72, 6); box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">含辞未吐，气若幽兰，华容婀娜。</span></p>
	<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">

	<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">但是曹植和甄宓之间，真心是什么事也没有的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">史学界一般共认，曹叡的血统是可疑的。但是这个锅，并不是袁熙背。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹丕费尽心机地争太子，然后曹丕篡汉，当上了魏文帝。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这个辛苦皇帝，寿只有七年。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">七年之中，司马懿进入中枢，开始带兵，最终成了&ldquo;许昌留守&rdquo;。类似&ldquo;荀彧</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&mdash;</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹操<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&rdquo;</span>最信任的关系。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹丕死时，留下遗诏，让他最信任的</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; color: rgb(2, 30, 170); box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹真，曹休，陈群，司马懿</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">，作为曹叡的&ldquo;辅命大臣&rdquo;，地位十分尊崇。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">可以说，曹丕一辈子，<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; color: red;">都把司马懿当成了最好的朋友</span>。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹叡的寿也不长，魏明帝13年，死于239年。这一年司马懿60岁。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹叡遗诏，让</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; color: rgb(2, 30, 170); box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹爽，司马懿</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">一起当&ldquo;辅政大臣&rdquo;。司马懿又一次成了托孤。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">辅佐养子曹芳。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">故事如果进行到这里，司马懿就是一个&ldquo;四朝老臣&rdquo;。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">地位高度尊崇，但在历史上也车载斗量。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">并没有特殊之处。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">二）反贼司马懿</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 21px; line-height: 31.5px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; color: red;">然后，司马懿就谋反了。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 21px; line-height: 31.5px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; color: red;">司马懿居然谋反了。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 21px; line-height: 31.5px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; color: red;">司马懿居然谋反了！</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">司马懿谋反，是历史上最奇特，最不合逻辑，最不可思议的事情。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">一个70岁的老人，在离开了政坛10年之后，居然倒戈一击。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">司马懿是&ldquo;四朝老臣&rdquo;，二度托孤。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">曹芳登机后，曹爽不爽司马懿，把<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; color: rgb(112, 48, 160);">爷爷辈的心腹</span>踢出朝堂，独霸朝政。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">司马懿也乖顺地象绵羊，丝毫没有和曹家人争。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">就这样足足</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 24px; line-height: 36px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; color: blue;">过了十年</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体_GB2312; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; widows: 1; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
