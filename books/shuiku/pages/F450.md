<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">为什么呢。因为“A股投资者”是按照3%的资金成本，来计算50元/股的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">对于那些苦哈哈的山西县城投资者，他们是很容易忽悠的。因为他们的人生中，除了3%没有更高的投资标的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">这根本就是二个世界，二个人生。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">中国的股市，从他诞生那天，就是充满泡沫的。几乎从1989年第一批“国企解困”上市，股市就是500%以上虚高。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">研究中外股市对比的人，永远搞不清楚。为什么中国会有50倍，100倍，1000倍额市盈率。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">其背后，是14%的通货膨胀，和3%的银行储蓄利率。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">通常情况下，这种“困局”如何解开。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">如果是完全的“市场经济”自由经济。按“<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; color: red;">IPO则大涨</span>”，如此巨大的套利空间，一定会刺激大量的企业拼命上市，增加供应，拉低价格，扯高回报。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">而在中国，政府不喜欢“IPO开闸”。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">政府希望维持着：IPO价格=5倍实际价值，希望维系着这样一个价格体系。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">一方面，IPO的都是国企。政府的脸谱是卖方。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">另一方面，“股民”已经形成了一个政治团体。股价大跌要闹的。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">所以你看中国的股市历史，就是不停的“IPO停发”。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">圈钱的多了，股票就大跌。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">股票跌了，就停IPO，停配股，停融资。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">而银行业改革，创新+反腐+确权，这些绝不是一朝一夕的事。积重难返。</span></p>
<ul class="list-paddingleft-2" style="margin: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-left: 30px; max-width: 100%; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 25.6px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">银行要创新。丰富产品线，并增加对贷款客户业务的理解。</span></p>
	</li>
	<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">银行要确权。对铁公基说No，银行不是政府的提款机。</span></p>
	</li>
	<li style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">银行要反腐。每一亿坏账判一个死刑。</span></p>
	</li>
</ul>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">你只要看一下这个清单。就知道这事办不成。没有几十年艰苦卓绝的改革，根本改不好。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">“加息”只需要行长一句话。但却是吗啡，根本不治病。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">“银行内部改革”是几十年滴水穿石的事。但却是真正的做实事。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">所以，“负利率”环境绝不可能改变。</span></p>
<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(62, 62, 62); font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, 'Hiragino Sans GB', 'Microsoft YaHei', Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-family: 楷体; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">改革没有捷径。</span></p>
