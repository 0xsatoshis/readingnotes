			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">在能够不受限制的利用这一能力时，它就能开启史无前例的&ldquo;无本万利&rdquo;之神奇能力&mdash;&mdash;这也是官僚和商业组织最大的不同。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">这里再次强调，这只是必须有和不必须有的关系，商业组织不必须有但不一定没有，事实上商业组织内也一定会有官僚。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">由于信息传递系统的漏洞，或者传递成本较高以至于管理成本很高所以只能放任，此时商业组织内部也会产生不同大小和数量的官僚组织。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">商业组织内部中层就有很多官僚，中低层也有，他们都是大量运用了自己的创造迷雾和干涉结果的&ldquo;技能&rdquo;。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">通常情况下，商业组织内部这类官僚是越少越好的，但高层一般对下方还是官僚&mdash;&mdash;因为这是本组织内权力的顶点（高度控制力没介入以前）。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">这里就明白了吧？商业组织也有官僚，但不是绝对有，即便没有也是可以活命的。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">政府是必须要有官僚的，因为政府本身就是顶点，而顶点必然是官僚。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">用简单的例子来说，一个公司，他有50个人，这50个人即便全体都知道整体意图，并且信息全部公开，它依然有可能可以活下来&mdash;&mdash;但是，他们不能独立存在，所以会发生分裂和分工。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">但是，一个政府，它有50个人，如果信息全部公开，知道整体意图，那么它一定会瓦解（可能会变成40个普通人，或者说商业组织，10个人由于早期的干涉形成了势力，可以干涉全体，于是成为了这40个人的&ldquo;政府&rdquo;）。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">当然，在现实中由于不平衡的广泛存在，而且总会有可能突然密集的发生在一小撮人中，这一小撮人也可能刚好&ldquo;关系&rdquo;不错，所以&ldquo;政府&rdquo;的雏形必然会出现，不可避免。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">那末，政府在现实世界中起到的最大作用是什么呢？或者说，官僚起到的最大作用是什么？</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">我的答案可能会有点奇怪。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">政府的最大作用，就是用来压制进步力量&mdash;&mdash;</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; color: rgb(255, 0, 0); font-size: 16px;">进步力量是指某种可能更有效的控制律和运行规律。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">那就奇怪了，这不就是说政府毫无存在意义了么？</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">不见得，尤其是考虑到社会控制方式模糊性的时候尤其如此。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">官僚本身的压制行为，实际上就是防止这些十分有效，但不一定适合大型组织的控制律过快的上升，导致整体组织的失控（实际上即便是合适的，过快的增长也可能导致问题）。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">我举个例子，就拿永久的&ldquo;不满意&rdquo;来说好了，比如说官僚的贪污腐败。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">贪腐本身，实际上就是一套新的控制律（交易也是一部分），去试图挑战旧有控制律的过程，不一定成功，但在这一局部，这种控制律的能力超过政府最高层给予的控制。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">如果这一腐败变得很强大，控制律很强，那么它就很有可能会&ldquo;正常化&rdquo;。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">我们可以想想我们现在的一些常见的行为，例如&ldquo;招商引资&rdquo;等等，这些事情正在原来的话毫无疑问是超级重罪（超级超级的贪腐），可是这么严重的一个&ldquo;贪腐&rdquo;，现在却不再是了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">这里就很明显，不是所有的&ldquo;贪腐&rdquo;方式都是能够升级成为一种全局策略的&mdash;&mdash;很显然，大部分贪腐本身会创造出更多的迷雾，从而诞生出更多的官僚，甚至商业机构本身也会被带着更官僚。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">剥削行为，并非发生在规模经济，资本利得等等上，剥削的核心，我个人觉得是官僚而不是商人。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 24px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-family: 楷体; line-height: 24px; font-size: 16px;">在一定历史时期，由于资本主义发展速度极快，所以市场平衡远远没有到来，因此大量的商业组织本身的&ldquo;渗透&rdquo;速度极快，于是大量地方产生了新的信息迷雾，促使了商业机构变得更像商人&mdash;&mdash;商人也更多的涉足了政治。</span></p>
