		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">当伊斯兰的学者想要研究星相时，他们被哈里发阻止了。因为这样的行为不“符合”真神的教义。</span></p>

		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">

			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">如果不嫌误导的话，请让我简单概括下淑娟阿姨的观点。他写了几十篇文章，但核心观点基本如下:</span></p>

			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">我们讨论生物多样性，一定不能忽略他生存环境的大前提。沼泽地里才能进化出青蛙，深海才能进化出鲸鱼。换一个环境鲸鱼绝对不可能有肺。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">同样道理，我们一个大前提：“在长达3000年的岁月里，中国一直以为自己是地球上唯一的文明”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这是一个大前提，而当皇帝消灭掉他视线内最后一个对手，国土直达大海的尽头。他选择儒家，而不是基督自治来治国。就顺理成章的了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">所以当1840年，英国人打进来的时候，整个儒家系统一下子傻了眼了。严复说：“二千年未有之大变局”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">的确，当时中国人面临的冲击，大概只有外星人登陆地球可以比拟了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">在几次战败之后，中国人面临痛苦的转型。不想当亡国奴的话，就必须进行全面的变革。抛弃儒教等级制，有追求“稳定”转为“效率”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这一个转型，从1840-1990，可以说一百五十年都没有转过来。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">一开始是儒学的崩溃。千年孔子，刚开始是将士不信，后来是士大夫不信。最后光绪自己也不信了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">中日甲午战争之后，光绪在自己的日记中写道：“我本来以为孔孟之道，圣人之言，是千古不变的大道理。现在看来，孔孟之道错了”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">五）题外话</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">插一句题外话。1894年中日甲午海战之后，“孔学”在日本的地位一落千丈。汉学家纷纷被扫地出门。日本走上了“全盘西化”的道路。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">说起日本文明，总感觉怪怪的。一方面，日本也是中华文化圈的一员，唐诗，寺庙，忠孝，这些都学得很齐全。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">另一方面，日本绝对不是一个“儒教”国家。至少不是一个正统的纯粹的儒教国家。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">熟读日本历史的人都知道，在日本，从来没有发生过中国这样“自上而下”的等级制。也没有大规模的科举。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">更没有为了追求稳定，把儒生上上下下塞满朝堂。弄得全紫绶都是“百无一用”的书生。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">为什么，为什么日本派遣了八次遣唐使，把建筑，宗教，诗文，堪舆，全部都学得十足。可就是没学诚中原文化的核心：“儒学”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这个问题其实不难回答。我们放眼四周，中国周边的诸多小国，高丽，日本，越南，吐蕃，蒙古，女真，回鹘，高昌。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">除了“高丽”这个不争气的小兄弟，其他虽然是儒学文化圈。但没一个是真正的儒国。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">因为儒学的要求。是大金字塔型。儒教国家很稳定，但是缺乏效率，英雄豪杰被论资排辈压制。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这样的结构，对于亚洲唯一大国，中原皇朝是适用的。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">可是你小国学什么学。小国一力追求稳定，放弃效率。你是不是准备被人一口吞了！</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">所以中国周边的国家，都是半桶水的儒家。在一半稳定的同时，小心翼翼维持着一半的效率。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">如果你想看看实例，翻翻越南史就可以了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">日本本身并不是一个彻底纯粹的儒家国家。当1853年美国“黑船”来到日本时，日本迅速地转身，第一个发起“明治维新”。也就不足为奇了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">六）中国的转身</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">说回正题。中国在1840年的转身，就要比日本困难得多。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">中国本身是一个等级制的国家。现在要转型成为一个“商业，契约，公民，费尔泼赖”的国家，整个中国人不适应。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">在最初的几十年，中国的表现非常地差。1840-1874是拒绝改革。同治中兴之后洋务运动，此后战败，战败，辛亥革命，连续的内战。大跃进，文ge，一直到1978年才算稍微消停。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">中华民族大约用了150年才完成了转身。但此后的表现并不差。目前已经成了全球第二大GDP，或许也是第二大强国。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">远远把老牌帝国主义英国，法国，德国，日本，全部都抛在了身后。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">我们用了这么大的篇幅来说这一件事。是为了解释冬川豆老师的第一条假设不对：</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">“西方文明远优于中国，中国应该学习西方政体”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">1840</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">年中国的孱弱，是一种异乎寻常的非常态。40000万中国人，打不过3000名英军士兵，也是一种异常的非常态。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">但是当中国扛过了第一轮的攻击，清醒恢复状态，中国的表现并不差。，</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>

			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">内陆省份不需要战备。如果绍兴，杭州，苏州，南京，每一个府都有3000甲士日夜不停的操练。这才是真正的浪费。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">你以为五十三人可以劫掠八个府，杀死四五千厢军。你把这数字再增加100倍，岂不是5300人可以消灭四五十万军队，可以灭了整个国了。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">事实的真相是，40年以后中国和日本发生了正面冲突。丰臣秀吉统一全日本，狭百战虎狼之师，登陆朝鲜。是所谓“壬辰倭乱”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">但是令太阁万万没想到的是，明军方面，只派了50000兵马入朝，就把日本倾全国之力的15万大军打得稀巴烂。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">当时日本缺铁，士兵穿的是所谓 “竹甲”。而明军是一支武装到牙齿的军队，在正面战场中，日军根本不敢和骑兵会战。小西行长唯一能做的事就是后退，后退，再后退。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这是一段详尽的历史。有兴趣的人，可以去看马伯庸·汗青写的《帝国最后的荣耀——大明1592·抗日援朝》<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; font-size: 10px; line-height: 18px; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">[3]</span></span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">这说明什么呢，《巍巍帝国，一触即溃》是一篇高度<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; text-decoration-line: underline; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">断章取义</span>的文章。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">他所描叙的明军“软弱，腐败，混乱，色厉内荏”，并不符合事实的真相。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">真相是，40年后，明国又老了一点。可是依然只靠50000兵力，就把丰臣秀吉的倾国大军打得稀巴烂。最后一直把老头气死为止。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">明军是一支强有力的军队。明军也是中国历史上，少有的“从开国到灭国”，一直都能保有战斗力的军队。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">如此污蔑明军，实属不该。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">如果要说战斗民族的话，冬川豆再三强调“自由的民族才是战斗民族”。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">说实话，我对斯巴达300勇士的故事，也是不太信的。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">而且即使自由能促使军队的精炼，英德军队素质始终优于苏联红军。他也不一定是可取的。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">毕竟最终失败的是（超精锐）德国党卫军，而不是苏军。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">为什么，请看下节。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">八）创新的价值</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">说到冬川豆老师的第三段逻辑链。“小国自治”容易发生工业革命。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">英国以地处边陲的一个小岛，一跃成为世界的主人，工业革命自然是不可缺少的决定性力量。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">一般的学术研究认为，“小国+大宪章+自治”，是工业革命产生的前提。因为在中国如果发生这类革新的话，皇帝会把它抢到手里。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">我无意在这件事上和冬川豆老师争执。我想说的是，即使这样，革新仍然不是选择“小国自治”的理由。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">因为生存是一场博弈。是一场冷血残酷，生死狡诈的战斗。并不一定是具有最大生产者，最光明仁慈伟大的人，才可以活到最后。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">在文明的博弈，在国家的博弈，在民族的博弈之中，并不一定就是文明的民族战胜野蛮民族。英雄战胜小人。自由战胜奴役。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">今天的英吉利全部都是AngloSaxon人的后裔，还有谁怀念亚瑟王呢。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">很多人都在谈论工业革命。可是他们没有说道的是，这一段光荣仅维持了120年左右。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">更确切点说，工业革命热跑，起步于1750年的英国。这期间英国的国力增长了约三倍，一跃由一个二流农业国变成欧洲最强大的国家。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">但1750</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 微软雅黑, sans-serif;">~</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">1870</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">年，仅仅120年之后，英国的光荣就不行了。此后的荣誉属于德国。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">德国从1870年开始工业化，用了大约二十到三十年的时间，在所有工业指数都超过了老牌英国。并短暂成为了世界第一GDP。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			&nbsp;</p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">而此时，德国刚刚由威廉一世加冕，由普鲁士大公国进阶成为德意志帝国。其背后的代价，是一连扫平了汉莎，黑森林，巴伐利亚，萨克森，巴登等五大诸侯国。</span></p>
		<p style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 25.5px;">
			<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 16px; line-height: 24px; font-family: 楷体;">其他林林总总的小侯爵领，更是被消灭了数之不尽。</span></p>
